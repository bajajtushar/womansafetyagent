Call for Help and Register Complaint

  • Domestic Violence/Abuse: Physical or emotional harm inflicted by a partner or family member.
  • Sexual Harassment or Assault: Unwanted sexual advances or forced sexual acts.
  • Emotional or Mental Distress: Anxiety, depression, extreme stress, or trauma.
  • Stalking or Threatening Behavior: Persistent harassment or intimidation in-person or online.
  • Financial Exploitation: Being coerced or manipulated into giving up money or assets.
  • Human Trafficking: Being forced or deceived into exploitative work or sexual activity without freedom to leave.
